# Privacy Policy for TuneMate

Last updated: February 24, 2026

TuneMate ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how the TuneMate mobile app (package ID: `com.benmonley.tunemate`) handles information.

## Summary

- TuneMate does not require account creation.
- TuneMate does not sell personal data.
- TuneMate does not use analytics, advertising SDKs, or crash reporting services.
- Audio input is processed on-device for tuning functionality.
- Custom tunings and app settings are stored locally on your device.

## Information We Access

### Microphone Audio

TuneMate requests microphone access so it can detect pitch and provide tuning feedback.

- Purpose: real-time instrument tuning
- Processing: on-device
- Storage: not stored by TuneMate
- Sharing: not shared by TuneMate

### Locally Stored App Data

TuneMate stores the following data locally on your device using app storage:

- app settings (for example theme mode, tuner behavior settings)
- custom tunings you create/import

This data remains on your device unless you remove it in-app, clear app data, or uninstall the app.

### Shared Tuning Codes

If you choose to share a tuning code, TuneMate passes that code to Android/iOS sharing interfaces selected by you (for example messaging or email apps). The data then follows the privacy practices of the app/service you share it with.

## Permissions

TuneMate currently requests:

- `RECORD_AUDIO`: required to detect musical pitch from microphone input
- `MODIFY_AUDIO_SETTINGS`: used to support audio capture behavior for tuning

## Data Sharing

TuneMate does not transmit your personal data to TuneMate-operated servers because TuneMate does not use a backend service.

TuneMate may interact with platform services needed to run app features (for example app distribution infrastructure or OS-level sharing), but TuneMate does not independently sell or broker your personal information.

## Data Retention and Deletion

- Microphone audio is processed in memory and is not retained by TuneMate.
- Stored settings and custom tunings remain on-device until deleted.
- You can remove app data by:
  - deleting custom tunings in-app
  - clearing app data in device settings
  - uninstalling the app

## Children's Privacy

TuneMate is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

## Security

TuneMate relies on device-level and platform-level protections. No method of storage or transmission is 100% secure, but we take reasonable steps to minimize data exposure within the app's design.

## International Users

Because TuneMate currently processes and stores app data locally on your device without a TuneMate backend, cross-border data transfer by TuneMate is generally not performed.

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be posted at the policy URL with a revised "Last updated" date.
